Buddha Institute of Technology, Gorakhpur also known as BIT, Gorakhpur, established in 2009, is a private degree engineering college situated in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, India. It offers undergraduate and post-graduate engineering programs in electronics and communication engineering, mechanical engineering, civil engineering, information technology and computer science.[1][2][3]
